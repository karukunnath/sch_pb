Welcome to Prabhath
Residential School

Prabhath Residential Public School is a coeducational institution affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education ( CBSE ), New Delhi. PRPS is situated at Karimugal nearly 3 km. away from Info Park Kochi and 5 km. from Kochi Refinery.

Established in 2000, PRPS provides quality education by providing Information Communication Technology enabled learning, Digital Classrooms, High Quality laboratories, Library etc. Hostel facilities are available for both boys and girls in separate campus. Extensive training is provided in arts and sports and our students secured several prizes in Inter-school and CBSE competitions.
